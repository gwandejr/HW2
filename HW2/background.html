Background

Education

I have a Bachelors Degree in Business from Cleveland State University where I majored in Finance. I received my Graduate Degree in Business from Indiana Wesleyan University. I'm currently attending Cuyahoga Community College.

Experience

I've worked in Mortgage Banking for almost 19 years mainly as a Financial Reporting Analyst. I specialized in production and profitability statistics on the origination side of the mortgage business. My strengths revolve around querying data warehouses, producing management reports, and streamlining and automating report processes from an end user perspective.

Some Accomplishments Include:

Data Expertise

Being the author of production statistics for the Mortgage Division of the company where the totals I derived were published in the company financials and peer group surveys, and was used for licensing and regulatory audits.

Data Integrity

Completely overhauled the report process used to report application volume to the Mortgage Bankers Association (MBA) that was in place before I started working for the company. After extensive research and testing, discovered erroneous data being reported and data being missed due to business process changes, timing, and outdated logic. As a result of the changes I implemented, produced a more accurate data set that didn't change over time and could be reconciled when looking back. This allowed for more accurate industry data forecasts which impacts budgeting.

Sales Metrics

Created and maintained a process that switched the Sales Incentive Methodology from being profitability-based to being volume-driven that incentivized for not only the amount of business a client was bringing in but also how many new clients with business were coming on board.

Automation

Created an end-user overnight batch job process using Microsoft Access and Excel where numerous daily reports would run automatically updating with new production and available before the start of business.
