My Career Journey

My name is Gary Anderson. I was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio where I still reside today. I've worked mainly in the banking industry for all of my career. With the Financial Crisis of 2008, the financial services industry was greatly impacted making it challenging to maintain job security. We're also seeing the increase of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and automation shift the job market from traditional labor jobs to more technical ones. It's imperative that one adopt a lifelong commitment to learning to combat these shifts.

Throughout my career I demonstrated a novice understanding of coding through end user computing within Microsoft Office products. I started researching computer programming as a way to increase my technical skills. I came across a program at Cuyahoga Community College called Cleveland Codes. It's a five-day-per-week, 16 hour-per-day course where you can earn a computer programming certification. Below is a schedule for the Cleveland Codes program that models a typical day.
